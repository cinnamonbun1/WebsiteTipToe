About us

Welcome to TipToe Soles, your destination for affordable and comfortable footwear. Based in Sydney, Australia, we have a rich history of manufacturing high-quality shoes, providing white-label products to various brands. Now, we are excited to bring our own brand to the market, offering a range of comfort-focused daily wear shoes specifically designed for customers aged 30 and above.

At TipToe Soles, we believe that comfort and affordability should go hand in hand. Our mission is to become the go-to footwear brand in Australia and around the world. We are committed to delivering stylish, durable, and eco-friendly shoes that cater to your daily needs.

Explore our collection and experience the perfect blend of comfort and style with TipToe Soles.
